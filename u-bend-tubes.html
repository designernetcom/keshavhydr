U-Bend Tubes

As the exclusive distributor of Sainest Tubes Pvt. Ltd. in Maharashtra, we offer clients direct access to their premium range of high-quality U-bend pipes and tubes. Renowned for their precision engineering and exceptional durability, these tubes are widely used in high-performance and high-temperature applications.

Our U-bend tubes are manufactured in compliance with TEMA Class 3 RCB standards or tailored to specific customer requirements. Each tube undergoes precise heat treatment processes and is subject to hydro testing, dye penetrant testing, and other quality checks to ensure mechanical integrity and dimensional accuracy. They are ideal for critical heat transfer operations in boilers, condensers, superheaters, and chillers.

Made from corrosion-resistant and high-strength materials, these tubes guarantee enhanced reliability and a longer service life, even under continuous exposure to extreme pressure and thermal cycles. On request, we can supply U-bend tubes with a bend radius of R = 1 x OD, providing compact design solutions for tight system configurations.

Applications

  • • Heat Exchangers – Power plants, refineries, and process industries
  • • Boilers – Superheated and saturated steam systems
  • • Condensers – Steam and process gas condensation
  • • Super Heaters – High-temperature steam generation
  • • Coolers and Inter-coolers – Industrial chilling plants
  • • Defense – Armaments and gun manufacturing
  • • Machine Tools – High-precision applications
  • • Pressure Vessels – Secure and high-pressure systems

Key Features

  • Material Grades: Carbon steel, alloy steel, stainless steel (as per requirement)
  • Manufacturing Standard: TEMA Class 3 RCB / Custom specifications
  • Radius of Bend: R = 1 x OD and as per design need
  • Heat Treatment: Fully normalized or stress relieved
  • Testing: Hydro testing, Dye penetrant testing, PMI on request
  • Finish: Bright annealed, pickled, or passivated
  • End Conditions: Plain, beveled, or threaded as per order
