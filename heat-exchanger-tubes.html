Heat Exchanger Tubes

These tubes are designed to efficiently transfer heat between fluids, ensuring optimal thermal performance across a wide range of industrial applications. Manufactured using high-quality carbon or alloy steel, they are built to withstand extreme conditions and are especially suited for demanding environments such as power plants, petrochemical refineries, chemical processing units, and various thermal systems. Their robust construction enables excellent resistance to high temperatures, pressure fluctuations, and corrosive elements. Our heat exchanger tubes are engineered for long service life, minimal maintenance, and seamless integration into complex heat transfer systems, making them a reliable choice for both new installations and replacement needs.

  • High thermal conductivity
  • Excellent corrosion resistance
  • Suitable for seamless welding
Specification Grades
ASTM : A 179, A 192, A 210 (Grade A1, C), A 334 (Grade 1, 6), A 556 (Grade-A2-B2-C2), A 106 (Grade A, B, C)
BS 3059 : Part I (Grade 320), Part II (Grade 360, 440), BS 3602 PT-1, CFS 360, BS-980 (CDS-1)
DIN 17175 : ST35.8, ST45.8, 15 MO3, 10CrMo44, 13CrMo910

Applications

  • • Heat exchangers are essential components used to transfer heat between two or more fluids without mixing them
  • • Boiler tubes are designed to withstand extreme pressure and temperature, making them essential components in steam generation systems
  • • Condenser tubes are specially designed to condense process fluids or steam by transferring heat to a cooling medium, typically air or water
  • • Super heater tubes are engineered to withstand extreme temperatures and pressures, making them essential for converting saturated steam into superheated steam in boilers
  • • Cooler and inter-cooler tubes are specifically designed for heat exchange applications in low-temperature systems such as chilling plants, refrigeration units, and air compression systems.
  • • Tubes used in armaments and gun manufacturing demand the highest level of precision, strength, and durability.

Technical Specifications

  • Outer Diameter: 3.0 mm to 177 mm
  • Wall Thickness: 0.5 mm to 15 mm
  • Length Range: Up to 30 meters
